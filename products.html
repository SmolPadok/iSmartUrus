ARSprecision© : Smart Body Temperature Screening and Sanitizing Station

ARSprecision© was developed by UTM researcher based on Cloud & AI technology, equipped with an infrared sensor which will detect the body temperature in real-time and data recording via QR-code platform. The device can replace manual temperature measurement and registration process, increasing screening efficiency. Supported with data management platform and QRcode function for easy integration with existing staff attendance and visitor management system. 9 key features customized to user’s needs:

  1. Automatic hand sanitizing dispenser
  2. Contactless temperature measurement
  3. High temperature alarm
  4. Foot disinfectant point
  5. Facial thermal detection
  6. Data management
  7. Attendance & visitors monitoring
  8. Daily analytics & real-time statistics
  9. Entrance door access control

CabinClear© : Automatic Cabin Vehicle Sanitizing System

CabinClear© is an automatic cabin vehicle sanitizing system. Disinfecting interior vehicles including passenger seats and other touchpoints by effectively misting disinfectant solution. Equipped with a one-touch switch button automatically sanitizing process without operator.

  • Safer for driver and passenger
  • Time efficient - avoid tedious wiping or manual spraying.
  • Fast installation, safe and cost effective customized to any type of vehicle - taxi, MPV, van, bus etc.

ADC : Automated Disinfectant Chamber

Automated Disinfection Chamber (ADC) ensuring fast and efficient disinfecting trolleys, baggage, parcels or consumer goods. Durable structure, easily relocated and can be built and customized with existing conveyors.

Counter Shield : Portable Counter-top Protective Barrier (CPB)

The CPB is a personal protective barrier that shields airport personnel from direct exposure to the customers they’re dealing with. The acrylic body can be mounted on most counter-tops and functions to minimize the spread of germs and viruses (including COVID-19) by separating the spaces between individuals.

  • Made of sturdy and transparent acrylic, 3/16” thick on stainless steel base, both surfaces smooth and easy to disinfect.
  • Suitable on any counters, from ticketing counters to check-in, flight transfers and boarding gates.
  • Customized shapes and sizes, on-site measurements, and installation services available upon request.
  • Freestanding countertop shields help stop the spread of germs and viruses, including coronavirus (Covid-19)
