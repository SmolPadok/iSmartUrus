About iSmartUrus

Our overview and what we strive for.

Company Profile

iSmartUrus is a Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) spin-off company, incorporated on 4th August 2013 for the sole purpose of commercializing UTM’s innovative product SMSParkir© (a novel end-to-end mobile payment solution) with the assistance from the Cradle Fund under Ministry of Finance and Agensi Inovasi Malaysia under the Prime Minister's Office.

The organization’s administrative base in Iskandar Puteri, Johor Bahru, home for many other tech startups created a community and an environment where the company can thrive in the organization’s technological base and infrastructure. Here we leverage on the area’s state-of-the-art networks, high-end research facility and ICT infrastructure to enable us to serve our clients with excellence.

iSmartUrus was established by experienced researchers and qualified professionals in electrical and electronics engineering. The company is also supported by a number of expert consultants in AI, robotics, process control and automation, renewable energy, energy management, mechanical electrical, ICT and telecommunication solution practitioner.
