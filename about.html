Next-gen engineering is now.

iSmartUrus Sdn Bhd is a Universiti Teknologi Malaysia spin-off company incorporated on 4th August 2013. We are an established company with experienced researchers and qualified professionals in electrical and electronics engineering.

Our company is supported by a number of expert consultants in energy management, process control, automation, renewable energy, electrical and telecomunication.

Our Mission

To offer and support our customers with reliable technologies and innovative solutions to help them stay competitive in the world of interconnected technology.

To provide our clients with the highest degree of professionalism, delivering the right solutions through smart use of state-of-the-art technology, complying with their requirements while remaining cost-effective.

Our Policy

  • Satisfaction of the customers on the quality of services delivered as our main priority.
  • The services provided by our company complies to the quality and standards set by the government and industry.

Our Health, Safety and Environment Policy

  • Obeying all the rules regarding HSE Policies whenever we work.
  • Create and implement an effective system to reduce negative impact and distractions towards the company.
  • Ensuring the application of safe and healthy working culture and fair distribution of responsibilities among workers.
  • Ensuring the HSE Policy is applied and updated consistently alongside the company mission and vision.
  • Exercise awareness regarding HSE Policy towards all parties involved in our company.
